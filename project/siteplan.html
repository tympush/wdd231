Comic Comet

Site Name Reasoning: The world of comic books and super heroes is full of cathy names that involve alliteration. To fit the website's theme, the name had to be something fun and specific.

Site Purpose: The purpose of the site is to be a fun hub for super hero fans and to learn about new comic book characters and receive comic book recomendations.

  • What are the powerstats of each character?
